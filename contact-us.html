What Happens After You Contact Us

We keep the process simple. You get in touch, we confirm the details of the job, and we give you a free quote, either remotely based on photos you send through, or on-site for larger or more complex jobs. Once the quote is approved, we book a time that works for you, including after-hours and weekends. Every job is carried out by a licensed electrician, backed by our 100% satisfaction guarantee.
